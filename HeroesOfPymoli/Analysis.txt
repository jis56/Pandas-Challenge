Based off the data, some observable trends can be seen with the playerbase of Heroes of Pymoli:

1) The majority of the game's demographic is male (84%). However, players who identified as female or other/non-disclosed had a higher average purchase price than their male counterparts. Even so, male players spent in total spent more than $1550 on in game purchases.

2) The 15-19 and 20-24 age groups make up the biggest age demographic in the game. They also spend the most amount of money on in game purchases.

3) The item "Final Critic" has the highest purchase count as well as the highest total purchase value, making it the most lucrative in game item.
